Agro Processing & Manufacturing Survey

The World Bank, in partnership with Studio 19, conducted an assessment of the manufacturing and agro-processing ecosystem in Tanzania, in an effort to undertstand key enablers and drivers of innovation and entrepreneurship for those sectors.

This assessment included a survey, which concentrates on the enterprise founder's experience, among others, in setting up a business in Tanzania, challenges, educational background, mentorship, past work experiences... In particular, the survey was looking to find out how relationships help to fuel innovation and economic growth in Tanzania.

Who Are We.

Studio 19 Limited was born in 2015 as a creative media company that is focused on telling Africa's stories, with the emphasis on using creative media and technology, to express what is inherent in the African continent.
